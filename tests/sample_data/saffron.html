Saffron pudding

One favorite on the Christmas dessert table is the saffron pudding which has become a tradition in our family.



Recipe:
2.5 dl round grain rice
5 dl water
9 dl fat milk
1 g saffron crushed with 1 lump of sugar
1 dl sugar
2 dl chopped, shelled almonds
3 eggs

Let the rice and water bubble with the sauce pan lid on for about ten minutes. Then add the milk and the saffron which has been crushed and mixed with the lump of sugar. Boil it up and then let it simmer for about 45 minutes. Cool the rice down, add the chopped almonds and the sugar as well as the eggs. Bake in the oven at 200Â´C for about 30 minutes or until it gets lightly brown. Serve the pudding with whipped cream and a jam, preferably made of dewberries, blackberries or blueberries.
